Blue Roof Top

What will you choose between food and Fresh Air? I bet you cannot make a choice. Now, what if we say that we have a choice for you between a boring, four-walled, sealed dining restaurant and an open, fresh air zone, rooftop build restaurant?To experience an ultra-fine dining experience, head over to the ‘Blue Rooftop Cafe Restaurant Bistro’ in Ahmedabad.
₹1000 for two approx | Multicuisine

Unlocked

Unlocked has an outside seating space which is very pleasant in the cool evenings They serve Savory 'French Crepes' in both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options but the batter does have eggs so it is for eggetarian and not vegetarians The menu has gourmet teas and detox juices for the health conscious.
₹1100 for two approx | Continental

VarieTEA

This is a café and tea bar located in the basement off Prahlad Nagar Road. It has a huge screen where one can watch live sports from the comfort of large sofas while enjoying a lots of varieties of teas, coffees and cold beverages along with snacks. This is a vegetarian place. It serves Tea, beverages, Italian and North Indian food.
₹600 for two approx | Cafe

I Sandwich House

Located in S G Highway, I Love Sandwiches serves what else but, sandwiches along with tea and other beverages. It also has a host of other snacks. This place is open from 8 am to 1 am. It serves both vegetarian and non-vegetarian food. Here they have a huge screen to watch live sports on and comfortable sofas to watch matches over long hours.
₹900 for two approx | Sandwiches

Urban Chowk

Urban Chowk is located on the road - Rajpath to SP Ring Road. It is a very happening place. It has multiple food stalls, comprising of a variety of cuisines. Whether you crave for Chinese, or continental, or south Indian or north Indian - you will find all the options there. Plus, a nice sitting area and music!
₹650 for two approx | Fast Food

Mocha

Mocha cafe and bar is a chain of cafes which has two of its outlets in Ahmedabad. They are known for their coffee and their variety of desserts. They also serve pasta, pizza, panini, sizzlers and freshly pressed juices. You can visit this place with a group or alone and enjoy a cup of coffee in the outdoor seating area on a pleasant winter day.
₹1000 for two approx | Italian

Juggernaut

Juggernaut is one of the best and unique cafes in Ahmedabad for sports lovers. They have a huge screen for the sports freaks where they can enjoy the live screening of matches along with playing on the field yourself. The lighting is also dim and cozy.
₹900 for two approx | Fasto Food

London Bubble

Bringing “Bubble waffles” to India, London Bubble Co. is known for their artistic waffles, pancakes and desserts which are 100% vegetarian and Gluten free. They offer large varieties to choose from. A lot of fillings and dressings as well as a lot of options for your sweet-tooth.
₹300 for two approx | Desserts

Good Thymes

The Good Thymes is one of a kind Passionately created Lounge Cafe to Serve, Craft, Brew, Toss, Fry, Churn, Griddle, Shake, Smoothie, Bake, Whisk, Fresh Food & Beverages.It is one of the most sought-after lawn facilities in the city. It was set up with a view to providing an elegant and superior lawn space.
₹800 for two approx | Cafe

Cafe De Italiano

"Rise Up With Every Sip". World of delicious Italian cuisine, mouth watering freak shakes, desserts and a lot more.Café de Italiano, this café located at Bodakdev, Ahmedabad is an exemplary place to hangout with your friends and family.

DoubleTree By Hilton

Located on the lobby level, HOP is ideal for a quick cup of coffee or some refreshing coolers. Dine in and enjoy our friendly, café-style ambience, or grab-and-go with a variety of light bites including fresh sandwiches, baked goods, and desserts.

Dangee Dums

Dangee Dums is a quick service restaurant located in Dev Aditya Arcade, Thaltej, West Ahmedabad. It serves ice creams and desserts at an affordable rate. The staff is polite and helpful in assisting customers. Dangee Dums is a great place for your after-dinner cravings.

Arbre Restro Cafe

Arbre Cafe is a new cafe in Ahmedabad, what makes this cafe unique is that you can enjoy your meals amidst nature.
